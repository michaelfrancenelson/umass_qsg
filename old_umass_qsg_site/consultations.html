QSG Consultations

QSG offers free consultations and technical assistance to ECo graduate students and faculty in the areas of study design, analytical methodology and interpretation of results. The purpose of these consultations is to improve quantitative literacy in environmental science and conservation among graduate students and faculty in ECo. Consultations are for advice only – take it or leave it. For graduate students, QSG advice does not override the guidance and/or requirements of the thesis/dissertation advisory committee. Ultimately, the student consultee is fully accountable for all decisions made and thus must be able to fully explain and defend all quantitative aspects of their research.

In addition to free advice, a QSG graduate assistant is available to provide free R technical assistance as needed and deemed appropriate by QSG.

Who is Eligible for QSG Consultation?

All graduate students and faculty in ECo are eligible for consultations. However, the experience, expertise and focus of QSG is on ecology and conservation science. Individuals seeking advice in other realms of environmental science and conservation, for example dealing with the social sciences, resource economics, business management, etc., are probably better served elsewhere.

How to Schedule a QSG Consultation?

QSG consultations are available every Monday 2:00-4:00 p.m. in 304 Holdsworth Hall during the academic session. Consultations must be scheduled in advance; walk-ins between the hours of 2:00-4:00 are not allowed. To schedule a consultation, sign up on the consultations sign-up sheet outside of 304 Holdsworth at least one week in advance.

How to Prepare for a QSG Consultation?

  1. Sign up in advance for a consultation on the sign-up sheet outside of 304 Holdsworth.
  2. If you are a graduate student, consult with your major advisor as to the nature of your consultation and invite him/her to attend the consultation with you.
  3. Complete the QSG consultation form [Word version; pdf version] to the best of your ability; this is required for all consultations.
  4. Bring 6 copies of the completed consultation form with you to the consultation.
